OpenEncoder
  • Fast video and audio encoding with FFmpeg
  • Presets for popular formats like MP4, WebM, and MP3
  • Drag-and-drop queue and progress tracking
  • Windows, macOS, and Linux builds

What it does

OpenEncoder makes format conversion easy with a native desktop app interface. Add files, choose presets, and export video/audio without manual FFmpeg commands.

Why it exists

It is designed for creators and editors who want a modern, open-source encoder alternative to Adobe Media Encoder, while still relying on the proven FFmpeg engine.

Built with

Electron, React, Tailwind CSS, Zustand, FFmpeg, and fluent-ffmpeg.

Hosted on Vercel. Download the latest app release from GitHub Releases.
